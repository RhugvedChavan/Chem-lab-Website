About

Digital Chemistry Lab

Overview

The Digital Chemistry Lab is a research group at the University of Mumbai dedicated to advancing chemical science through computational and AI-driven approaches. The lab focuses on solving complex chemical problems using a combination of theoretical chemistry, data science, and machine learning.

Vision

To transform chemical research by integrating artificial intelligence and computational methods into mainstream scientific workflows.

Mission
  • Develop AI-driven tools for chemical analysis
  • Advance research in computational chemistry
  • Bridge academia and industry through technology
  • Train students in modern digital research techniques
