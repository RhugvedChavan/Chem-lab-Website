About

The Digital Chemistry Lab integrates computational chemistry, AI, and data-driven research. We pioneer next-generation tools for molecular discovery, reaction prediction, and spectral analysis, driving innovation at the intersection of digital intelligence and chemical sciences.

Key Innovation

We are developing Spectral-Hub, an AI-powered platform that transforms spectral data into real-time chemical insights.

  • Unmix-X: Spectral signal separation
  • Decode-X: Automated interpretation engine
  • Predict-X: Simulate Spectral Data

Impact

  • 🤖 Integration of AI into chemical research
  • 🎓 Training students in modern digital chemistry
